SecureNotes Help
================

Creating a Note
---------------
1. Log in to your account.
2. Click "New Note" in the navigation bar.
3. Enter a title and content for your note.
4. Click "Save Note".

Searching Notes
---------------
Use the Search page to find notes by keyword.
Results are filtered to your own notes only.

Uploading a Photo
-----------------
Navigate to the Upload page to attach a profile photo.
Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF.

Logging Out
-----------
Click "Logout" in the navigation bar to end your session.
Your session will also expire after 30 minutes of inactivity.
